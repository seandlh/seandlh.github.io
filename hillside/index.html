About

Hillside Academy is a homeschool devoted to joyful scholarship, character, and connection with the natural world. We blend classic academics with hands‑on projects, outdoor exploration, and creative arts.

Personalized Learning

Small group instruction and one‑on‑one mentoring ensure every learner is seen and supported.

Nature Days

Weekly field lessons in local parks and trails—journaling, sketching, identifying flora and fauna.

Character & Community

Service projects, practical life skills, and plenty of time to play and build friendship.

Curriculum

We follow a flexible, interest‑led approach anchored by core studies. Here’s a snapshot:

Language Arts

Reading & Writing

Read‑alouds, phonics, copywork, original stories, and author studies with nature themes.

STEM

Math & Science

Hands‑on experiments, math games, kitchen chemistry, and garden‑based investigations.

Humanities

History & Geography

Timelines, mapwork, biographies, and local history walks.

Arts

Art & Music

Watercolor nature journals, handicrafts, folk songs, and seasonal celebrations.

Outdoor Fridays: Pack boots and a sketchbook—every Friday we learn outside.
